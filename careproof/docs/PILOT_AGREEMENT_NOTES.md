# Pilot Agreement Notes

Use these points in the pilot agreement:

- 30-day pilot term
- Limited caregiver and client counts
- Agency is responsible for caregiver and family consent
- CareProof is for operational communication and proof of care, not diagnosis
- No emergency response guarantee
- Data export available at pilot end
- Founder pricing offered if pilot converts

Required disclaimer:

CareProof is not a substitute for emergency services, clinical judgment, or licensed medical care.
