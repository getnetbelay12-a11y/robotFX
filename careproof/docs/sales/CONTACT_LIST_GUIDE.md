# Contact List Guide

Use [CONTACT_LIST_TEMPLATE.csv](/Users/getnetbelay/Documents/New%20project/careproof/docs/sales/CONTACT_LIST_TEMPLATE.csv) as the working spreadsheet import.

## Required columns

- Agency Name
- Website
- City
- State
- Owner or Contact Name
- Email
- Phone
- LinkedIn
- Current Software Mentioned
- Family Update Pain Signal
- Outreach Date
- Follow-up 1
- Follow-up 2
- Response
- Demo Booked
- Pilot Fit Score
- Notes

## Pilot fit scoring

| Score | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| 5 | Perfect pilot candidate |
| 4 | Strong fit |
| 3 | Possible |
| 2 | Weak |
| 1 | Bad fit |

A `5` means:

- small or mid-size agency
- owner reachable
- family communication emphasized
- no heavy enterprise feel
- private-pay services

## Pain signals to note

- Mentions family-owned or locally owned
- Heavy focus on personal care or companion care
- No visible portal or family update workflow
- Website emphasizes trust, communication, or caregiver reliability
- Reviews mention missed updates, inconsistency, or poor communication
