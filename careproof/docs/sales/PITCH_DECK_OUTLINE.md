# CareProof Pitch Deck Outline

## Slide 1 — Cover

CareProof  
Prove every visit. Update every family. Catch care issues early.

Prepared by Kelel IT Solution

## Slide 2 — Problem

Home care agencies lose time and trust when families do not know what happened during visits.

## Slide 3 — The Trust Gap

Agency → Caregiver → Client → Family

When updates are manual, trust breaks.

## Slide 4 — Solution

CareProof creates a proof-of-care timeline for every visit.

## Slide 5 — Product Experience

One platform. Three simple experiences:

- Agency dashboard
- Caregiver mobile app
- Family update view

## Slide 6 — Agency Dashboard

See late visits, missed visits, incidents, family concerns, at-risk clients, and caregiver reliability.

## Slide 7 — Caregiver Mobile Workflow

- Today’s visit
- Start visit
- Complete checklist
- Add note
- End visit

## Slide 8 — Family Trust Experience

Families receive calm, professional updates without calling the agency.

## Slide 9 — Pilot Offer

- 30-day pilot
- Up to 10 caregivers
- Up to 20 clients
- Setup included
- Weekly review

## Slide 10 — Call to Action

Let’s run a controlled 30-day pilot.
