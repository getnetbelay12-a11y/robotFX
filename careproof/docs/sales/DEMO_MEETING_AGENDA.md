# CareProof Demo Meeting Agenda

## 20-minute structure

1. 2 minutes — Understand their current workflow
2. 3 minutes — Explain the trust gap
3. 8 minutes — Product demo
4. 3 minutes — Pilot offer
5. 3 minutes — Questions
6. 1 minute — Next step

## Questions to ask before demo

- How do families get updates today?
- How often do they call asking about visits?
- How do you know if caregivers are late?
- What software do you currently use?
- What is one thing you wish was easier?

Do not demo blindly. Tie the demo to their pain.
