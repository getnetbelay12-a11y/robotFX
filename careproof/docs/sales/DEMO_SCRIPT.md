# CareProof Demo Script

## Opening — 20 seconds

Most home care agencies already have scheduling or billing software. CareProof is different. It focuses on the trust gap after a caregiver visit starts: Did the caregiver arrive? What was done? Was there an issue? Did the family receive an update?

## Agency Dashboard — 40 seconds

This is what the agency owner or coordinator sees every morning: late visits, missed visits, open incidents, family concerns, and at-risk clients. The goal is not just reporting. It shows what needs action today.

## Caregiver App — 60 seconds

The caregiver opens the mobile app, sees today’s visits, starts the visit, completes a checklist, adds a short care note, reports any issue if needed, and ends the visit.

## Family Update — 40 seconds

After checkout, CareProof generates a clean family update. The family sees what tasks were completed and can submit a concern if something needs follow-up.

## Incident Review — 30 seconds

If there is a serious incident, it goes to agency review first. Raw sensitive details are not automatically pushed to families.

## Weekly Report — 20 seconds

At the end of the week, the agency can generate a professional care report.

## Close — 10 seconds

CareProof helps agencies prove visits, update families, reduce complaints, and catch care issues early.
