# CareProof Cold Call Script

Hi, my name is Getnet. I’m working on CareProof, a simple mobile tool for home care agencies.

It helps agencies prove caregiver visits, send family updates, and catch issues like late visits, missed tasks, or incidents earlier.

I’m not calling to replace your scheduling or billing software. The focus is reducing family update calls and improving caregiver accountability.

Can I ask: how do families currently get updates after visits?

## If they already use software

That makes sense. Many agencies do. CareProof is designed as a lighter family-trust layer, not a full replacement. The question is whether families are actually getting clear updates after visits without calling your office.

## If they say they have no problem

That is good. Then CareProof may not be urgent for you. Before I let you go, do families ever call asking if a caregiver arrived or what was done during the visit?
