# Discovery Questions

Ask these before or during the demo:

- How do families know a visit was completed?
- How often do families call asking for updates?
- How do caregivers document tasks today?
- What happens when a caregiver is late?
- How do you handle incidents?
- What software do you use now?
- What do you wish was easier?
- Would automated family updates help?
- Would proof-of-care reports help with trust?

Most important question:

If this reduced family update calls and improved caregiver accountability, would you consider paying for it?

Do not skip that question. Compliments are not validation.
