# 20-Minute Demo Call Flow

## Minute 0–3: Their workflow

Ask:

- How do your families get updates today?
- Where does communication break down?

## Minute 3–5: Position CareProof

Say:

CareProof is not trying to replace your current scheduling or billing software.

It focuses on the trust gap after the visit starts: caregiver proof, family updates, incident visibility, and weekly reports.

## Minute 5–13: Demo

Show only this flow:

- Admin creates visit
- Caregiver checks in
- Caregiver completes checklist
- Caregiver adds note
- Caregiver checks out
- Family receives update
- Agency sees proof timeline
- Weekly report generated

Do not show every screen.

## Minute 13–16: Pilot offer

Say:

For the pilot, we would start small:

- up to 10 caregivers
- up to 20 clients
- 30 days
- setup support included
- weekly review

## Minute 16–19: Success metrics

Say:

We would measure:

- visit completion through CareProof
- checklist completion
- care note completion
- family updates sent
- late and missed visits caught
- family concerns submitted
- whether update calls were reduced

## Minute 19–20: Close

Ask:

Would you be open to testing this with a small group of caregivers and families?

Do not end with “let me know.” Ask directly.
