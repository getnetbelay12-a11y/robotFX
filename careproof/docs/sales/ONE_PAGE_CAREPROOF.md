# CareProof

## Prove every visit. Update every family. Catch care issues early.

CareProof is a mobile-first proof-of-care and family update platform for home care agencies.

## The Problem

Home care agencies lose time and trust when families do not know what happened during visits.

- Families call repeatedly asking for updates
- Caregivers document visits inconsistently
- Late or missed visits are caught too late
- Incidents and concerns get buried in texts and phone calls
- Owners cannot easily see which clients are at risk

## The Solution

CareProof gives agencies a simple workflow:

Caregiver checks in  
→ completes care checklist  
→ adds care note  
→ reports issue if needed  
→ checks out  
→ family receives clean update  
→ agency sees proof timeline  
→ weekly report is generated

## Core Benefits

- Reduce family update calls
- Improve caregiver accountability
- Catch issues earlier
- Protect agency reputation
- Keep clients longer
- Generate professional weekly reports

## Who It Is For

- Private-pay non-medical home care agencies
- Agencies with 10–100 caregivers
- Small admin teams
- Agencies that want better family communication
- Agencies that need simple visit proof without heavy software
