# Pilot Kickoff Agenda

30-minute kickoff:

1. 5 minutes — Confirm pilot goals
2. 5 minutes — Confirm users and clients
3. 5 minutes — Explain workflow
4. 10 minutes — Admin and caregiver training
5. 3 minutes — Confirm success metrics
6. 2 minutes — Confirm next check-in

## Pilot goals

- Reduce family update calls
- Improve visit documentation
- Detect late and missed visits faster
- Improve family confidence
- Generate weekly reports
