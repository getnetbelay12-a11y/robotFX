# CareProof Demo Video Script

Length: 3 minutes max

## Opening — 20 seconds

Most home care agencies already have scheduling or billing software. CareProof is different. It focuses on the trust gap after a caregiver visit starts: Did the caregiver arrive? What was done? Was there an issue? Did the family receive an update?

## Admin dashboard — 40 seconds

This is the agency dashboard. The owner or coordinator immediately sees today’s visits, late visits, missed visits, open incidents, family concerns, and at-risk clients. The goal is not just reporting. It shows what needs action today.

## Caregiver app — 60 seconds

The caregiver opens the mobile app and sees today’s visits. They start the visit, complete a simple checklist, add a short care note, report any issue if needed, and end the visit. The workflow is intentionally simple so caregivers can document care quickly.

## Family update — 40 seconds

After checkout, CareProof generates a clean family update. The family can see what tasks were completed and submit a concern if something needs follow-up.

## Incident review — 30 seconds

If there is a serious incident, it goes to agency review first. Raw sensitive details are not automatically pushed to the family.

## Closing — 30 seconds

CareProof helps agencies prove visits, update families, reduce complaints, and catch care issues early. We are inviting a few agencies into a 30-day pilot.
