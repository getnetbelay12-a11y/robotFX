# CareProof Objection Handling

## “We already have software.”

That makes sense. CareProof is not trying to replace your scheduling or billing system. It focuses specifically on proof-of-care, family updates, incident visibility, and reducing family communication burden.

## “Caregivers are not technical.”

That is exactly why the caregiver app is simple: today’s visit, start visit, checklist, note, end visit. No complex menus.

## “Families will not download another app.”

They do not have to start with the app. The first version can send email or SMS updates. The family portal is optional for details and concerns.

## “Is it HIPAA compliant?”

CareProof is built with role-based access, audit logs, secure authentication, and agency-level data isolation. For formal HIPAA compliance or BAA requirements, we would review the agency’s workflow and compliance needs before expanding into regulated clinical use.

## “How much does it cost?”

The pilot is 30 days. After the pilot, plans start at $149/month for small agencies.

## “What if we stop using it?”

Your agency can export key records and reports. We do not lock you in.
