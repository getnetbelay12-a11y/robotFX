# CareProof Sales Qualification

## Good pilot agency

- 10–100 caregivers
- family update pain
- owner or admin still involved
- willing to try new software
- private-pay clients
- weak current family communication

## Bad pilot agency

- huge enterprise
- requires deep EVV integration immediately
- no family update pain
- owner is not involved
- wants free custom software
- cannot commit users to test

Do not waste time on pilots that have no operational pain and no internal champion.
