# CareProof First-Customer Execution Plan

## Objective

Get:

- 50 agency contacts
- 10 conversations
- 5 demos
- 1 pilot agreement

This is the first real validation.

## Ideal first customer

Target:

- Private-pay non-medical home care agency
- 10–100 caregivers
- Owner-operated or family-owned
- Weak family communication process
- Uses phone calls or texts for updates
- Not locked into enterprise software
- Located in U.S. metro or suburban areas

Best early states and cities:

- Maryland
- Virginia
- Washington DC
- Texas
- Georgia
- Minnesota
- Ohio
- Pennsylvania
- New Jersey
- California

## Where to find agencies

Use:

- Google Maps
- Yelp
- LinkedIn
- Facebook business pages
- State home care associations
- Local senior care directories
- Home Care Pulse listings
- Care.com provider search
- A Place for Mom provider listings

Search terms:

- home care agency near me
- non medical home care agency
- private duty home care
- senior care agency
- personal care agency
- companion care agency
- home care services for seniors

## Execution rules

- Score every agency 1 to 5 before outreach.
- Prioritize `5` and `4` first.
- Call and email in the same week.
- Do not spend time on agencies with no visible family communication pain.
- No owner access usually means low pilot probability.

## 30-day target

Week 1:

- Build the 50-agency list
- Send first outreach emails
- Make first call block

Week 2:

- Run demos
- Capture objections
- Tighten positioning

Week 3:

- Push one pilot candidate to decision
- Collect import data if they say yes

Week 4:

- Start pilot
- Review weekly metrics
- Ask directly about paid continuation

## Brutal truth

If agencies do not respond, the targeting or message is weak.

If they take demos but do not pilot, the value is still unclear.

If they pilot but do not pay, the product did not solve a painful enough problem.
