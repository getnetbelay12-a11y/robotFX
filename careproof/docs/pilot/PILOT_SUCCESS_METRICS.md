# CareProof Pilot Success Metrics

## Weekly operational report

- Visits completed through CareProof
- Checklist completion rate
- Care note completion rate
- Family updates sent
- Late visits detected
- Missed visits detected
- Incidents submitted
- Family concerns submitted
- Average response time
- Caregiver adoption rate

## Success thresholds

- 80%+ visits completed through CareProof
- 90%+ completed visits include checklist
- 85%+ completed visits include note
- 80%+ family updates sent successfully
- Agency says update calls were reduced
- Agency is willing to pay after pilot

## End-of-pilot question

Did CareProof reduce family update calls and improve caregiver accountability enough for you to pay for it?
