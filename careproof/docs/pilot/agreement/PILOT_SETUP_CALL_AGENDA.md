# CareProof Pilot Setup Call Agenda

## Goal

Define a small, controlled pilot with a named champion, a clear scope, and measurable success criteria.

## 30-Minute Structure

### 0-5 Minutes: Confirm Pilot Goal

Say:

The purpose of this call is to define a small, controlled pilot.

We are not trying to roll this out across the whole agency immediately. The goal is to test whether CareProof reduces family update calls, improves caregiver documentation, and gives your team a clearer proof-of-care timeline.

Ask:

Who on your team will own the pilot day-to-day?

### 5-10 Minutes: Confirm Users and Clients

Ask:

- How many caregivers should be in the first pilot?
- How many clients should be included?
- How many family members should receive updates?
- Are there any clients we should avoid in the pilot?

### 10-15 Minutes: Confirm Workflow

Review:

1. Agency imports caregivers, clients, family members, and visits
2. Caregiver logs in
3. Caregiver sees assigned visit
4. Caregiver checks in
5. Caregiver completes checklist
6. Caregiver adds care note
7. Caregiver reports issue if needed
8. Caregiver checks out
9. Family receives update
10. Agency reviews dashboard
11. Weekly report generated

Ask:

Which part of this workflow is most different from how your team works today?

### 15-20 Minutes: Confirm Success Metrics

Use:

- Visit completion rate through CareProof
- Checklist completion rate
- Care note completion rate
- Family updates sent
- Late/missed visits detected
- Family concerns submitted
- Caregiver adoption
- Family feedback
- Agency willingness to pay

Ask directly:

If the pilot hits these targets and reduces family update calls, would you be comfortable moving to a paid plan starting at $149/month?

### 20-25 Minutes: Confirm Onboarding and Data Import

Ask:

- Who will send the CSV files?
- Who will train caregivers?
- Who will respond to family concerns?
- Should families receive email, SMS, or portal-only updates?

### 25-30 Minutes: Confirm Start Date and Next Actions

Lock down:

- Pilot champion
- Start date
- Weekly review time
- Import owner
- Training owner
