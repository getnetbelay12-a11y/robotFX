# CareProof Pilot Success Scorecard

## Adoption

- [ ] 80%+ selected visits completed through CareProof
- [ ] 90%+ completed visits include checklist
- [ ] 85%+ completed visits include care note

## Family Communication

- [ ] 80%+ completed visits generate family update
- [ ] Families understand visit summaries
- [ ] Family concern submission works
- [ ] Agency reports fewer update calls

## Agency Operations

- [ ] Late/missed visits visible
- [ ] Incident queue useful
- [ ] Family concern queue useful
- [ ] Weekly reports useful
- [ ] Dashboard helps identify urgent work

## Business Decision

- [ ] Agency wants to continue
- [ ] Agency is willing to pay
- [ ] Agency wants to expand users/clients
- [ ] Agency will provide testimonial or referral
