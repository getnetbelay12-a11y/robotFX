# Day 21 Review Meeting Runbook

## Goal

Force a clear Week 2 decision:

- Continue same group
- Continue with coaching
- Reduce pilot group
- Pause and fix

Do not leave the meeting with vague language such as "looks good" or "let's see."

## Before The Meeting

Prepare:

- `docs/pilot/live/[agency_name]/WEEK_1_REVIEW.md`
- `docs/pilot/live/[agency_name]/DAY_21_REVIEW_NOTES.md`
- `docs/pilot/live/[agency_name]/WEEK_2_PLAN.md`

Also prepare the core numbers:

- Scheduled visits
- Started visits
- Completed visits
- Checklist completion percent
- Care note completion percent
- Family update percent
- Late and missed visits
- Incidents
- Family concerns
- Caregiver adoption by person
- Open issues

## 30-Minute Agenda

### 0-3 Minutes: Restate Pilot Goal

Say:

The goal today is to decide whether CareProof is helping with the two things we agreed to test: caregiver accountability and family communication.

I'll show the Week 1 numbers, what worked, what did not work, and then we should decide whether Week 2 should continue, expand, reduce, or pause.

### 3-8 Minutes: Review Week 1 Metrics

Show:

- Scheduled pilot visits
- Started visits in CareProof
- Completed visits in CareProof
- Checklist completion percent
- Care note completion percent
- Family updates generated percent
- Incidents submitted
- Family concerns submitted

### 8-13 Minutes: Review Caregiver Adoption

Focus on:

- who adopted fastest
- who struggled
- who needs coaching
- whether any caregiver should pause from the pilot

### 13-18 Minutes: Review Family Updates and Concerns

Ask:

- Did families receive useful updates
- Were any updates confusing
- Did concerns surface through CareProof
- Did this reduce any family update calls

### 18-23 Minutes: Discuss Issues and Fixes

Show:

- main issues
- root causes
- fixes already applied
- open blockers

### 23-27 Minutes: Decide Week 2 Plan

Choose one:

- Continue same group
- Continue with coaching
- Reduce pilot group
- Pause and fix

### 27-30 Minutes: Confirm Paid-Continuation Signal and Next Actions

Ask:

If Week 2 improves adoption and the family updates continue working, does continuing on the Starter plan at $149 per month feel reasonable?

If not:

What would need to be true by the end of the pilot for CareProof to be worth that price?
