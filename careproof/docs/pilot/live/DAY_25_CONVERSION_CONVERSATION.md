# Day 25 Conversion Conversation

## Framing

Say:

We are getting close to the point where we should decide whether CareProof is valuable enough to continue after the pilot.

For the next review, I'd like to walk through the pilot results, what value your team saw, what still needs improvement, and whether continuing on the Starter plan makes sense.

Then ask:

Can we include the person who would approve the paid continuation?

## Questions

1. What has been most useful so far?
2. What would your team miss if CareProof stopped tomorrow?
3. Did family update calls decrease at all?
4. Did caregiver documentation improve?
5. Did the dashboard help you catch anything earlier?
6. Which feature feels most valuable?
7. Which feature feels unnecessary?
8. What would stop you from paying after the pilot?

## Pricing Question

If Week 2 keeps improving and the family updates continue working, does the Starter plan at $149/month feel reasonable?

If not:

What would need to be true by the end of the pilot for CareProof to be worth that price?

## Rule

If the decision-maker is not in the room, you are not having a real conversion conversation.
