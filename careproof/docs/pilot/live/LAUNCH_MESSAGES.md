## Caregiver Launch Reminder

Hi [Caregiver Name],

Reminder: today your agency is starting the CareProof pilot.

For each assigned visit:

1. Open CareProof
2. Tap Today
3. Open your visit
4. Tap Start Visit
5. Complete the checklist
6. Add a short care note
7. Report an issue if needed
8. Tap End Visit

If you have trouble logging in or do not see your visit, contact [Pilot Champion Name] immediately.
