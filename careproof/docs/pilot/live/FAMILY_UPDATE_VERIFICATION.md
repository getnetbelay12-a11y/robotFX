# Family Update Verification

Check every completed visit you sample and confirm:

- [ ] Update was generated
- [ ] Update was sent to the correct family
- [ ] Update is understandable
- [ ] Update tone is calm
- [ ] Raw incident details are not exposed
- [ ] Internal notes are not exposed
- [ ] Concern button works

## Good Family Update

Today's visit was completed. Meal assistance, medication reminder, and mobility support were completed. The caregiver noted: No major concerns.

## Good Review-Case Update

Today's visit was completed and is being reviewed by the agency. The agency will follow up if needed.

## Bad Family Update

High severity incident: client fell in bathroom and caregiver called admin.
