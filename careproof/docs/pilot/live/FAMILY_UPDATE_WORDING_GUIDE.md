# Family Update Wording Guide

## Good Templates

### Normal Completed Visit

Today's visit was completed. The caregiver documented the selected care tasks and added a short note for the agency and family.

### Completed With Task List

Today's visit was completed. Completed tasks included: [tasks]. The caregiver noted: [safe note].

### Requires Agency Review

Today's visit was completed and is being reviewed by the agency. The agency will follow up if needed.

### Family Concern Confirmation

Your concern was sent to the agency. A team member will review it and follow up according to the agency's process.

## Avoid

- Patient condition declined
- Possible medical deterioration
- Emergency risk
- Diagnosis-like wording

CareProof is not a clinical diagnosis system.

## Safety Rule

Never let raw high-severity incident language reach families automatically.
