# Day 26 Decision-Maker Alignment

## Goal

Make sure the person who can actually approve payment is included in the continuation discussion.

If only the pilot champion attends and cannot approve payment, you are not closing. You are just presenting.

## Ask The Champion

Who would approve continuing CareProof after the pilot?

Possible roles:

- Agency owner
- Operations manager
- Administrator
- Finance or admin lead

## Invite Message

Hi [Name],

As we move toward the end of the CareProof pilot, the next review should include whoever would approve continuing the service after the pilot.

We will review:

- pilot usage results
- caregiver adoption
- family update performance
- operational value
- remaining gaps
- continuation options

The goal is to decide whether CareProof should continue after the pilot, whether we need a short extension with clear success conditions, or whether it is not the right fit.

Who should join that discussion?
