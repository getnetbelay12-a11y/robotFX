# CareProof 30-Day Pilot Proposal

## Executive Summary

CareProof helps home care agencies prove visits, update families, reduce complaints, and catch care issues early.

## Pilot Scope

Duration: 30 days  
Caregivers: up to 10  
Clients: up to 20  
Family members: invited by agency  
Support: setup and weekly review

## Included

- Admin dashboard
- Caregiver mobile workflow
- Family updates
- Visit proof timeline
- Incident queue
- Family concern queue
- Weekly reports
- CSV onboarding support

## Not Included

- Payroll
- Billing
- Medicaid EVV integration
- Clinical diagnosis
- Emergency response service

## Workflow

1. Agency schedules visit
2. Caregiver starts visit
3. Caregiver completes checklist
4. Caregiver adds note
5. Caregiver reports issue if needed
6. Caregiver ends visit
7. Family receives update
8. Agency reviews dashboard
9. Weekly report is generated

## Success Metrics

- Visit completion rate through CareProof
- Checklist completion rate
- Care note completion rate
- Family updates sent
- Late/missed visits detected
- Family concerns submitted
- Agency feedback
- Willingness to continue paid plan
