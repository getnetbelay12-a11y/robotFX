## What Failed

1. No outreach execution was logged from this workspace.
2. No reply, conversation, demo, or pilot signal was created.
3. The unresolved agency stayed active too long without a forced outcome.

## Biggest Blocker

Execution discipline. The business still has no first-contact data, so message quality, channel quality, and segment quality remain mostly untested.

## Best Tactic

Tactic:
Clear operating discipline that exposed the real blocker instead of hiding behind more planning.

Evidence:
By Day 63 and Day 64, the repo made it explicit that the issue was not product readiness or message theory. It was zero logged outreach execution.

Why it worked:
It prevented false optimism and forced a cleaner sprint decision.

How we scale it:
Use the same measured daily logging, but only alongside real sends, calls, and touched-row status updates.

## Tactic to Kill

Tactic:
Passive email-only outreach without same-day call pressure or touched-row updates.

Evidence:
Four days of sprint documentation still produced 0 emails, 0 calls, 0 conversations, and 0 demos from this workspace.

Replacement:
Call-first outreach to fit-score 4 and 5 agencies, followed by pain-based follow-up email and immediate tracker updates.
