# CareProof Month 2 Execution Plan

## Selected Track

- [ ] Track A — Paid customer / strong pilot
- [ ] Track B — Pilot but not paid yet
- [ ] Track C — Demos but no pilot
- [x] Track D — No demos yet

## Main Month 2 Goal

Book the first real discovery calls and demos with small private-pay home care agencies so CareProof gets real market signal instead of more internal theory.

## Top 3 Metrics

1. Agencies contacted
2. Conversation rate
3. Demo booking rate

## Product Priorities

1. Keep the demo environment stable across backend, admin, and caregiver mobile flows.
2. Fix only the workflow blockers that would break a live demo or pilot conversation.
3. Tighten the proof-of-care and family-update story so the product demo stays sharp and believable.

## Sales / Customer Success Priorities

1. Contact 200 agencies with disciplined outreach, not random bursts.
2. Make 80 calls with a pain-first script to learn what stops buyers.
3. Book 10 discovery calls or demos with owner/admin decision-makers.

## Do Not Build

- Payroll
- Billing
- EVV
- AI diagnosis
- Custom branding
- Advanced analytics
- Chat/video
- Marketplace

## Hard Truth

Month 2 does not need more architecture. It needs proof that agency owners care enough to talk, demo, and eventually pilot. If outreach volume and buyer conversations stay near zero, more product work is mostly self-entertainment.
