# CareProof Demo Recording Script

## Title

CareProof Demo — Prove Every Visit, Update Every Family

## Opening — 20 seconds

CareProof helps home care agencies prove visits, update families, reduce complaints, and catch care issues early. It is not designed to replace scheduling or billing software. It focuses on the trust gap after a caregiver visit starts.

## Agency Dashboard — 40 seconds

Show:

- Today’s visits
- Late visits
- Missed visits
- Requires review
- Open incidents
- Family concerns
- At-risk clients

Say:

The agency dashboard shows what needs attention today, not just charts.

## Caregiver Workflow — 45 seconds

Show:

- Today’s visits
- Start visit
- Checklist
- Care note
- End visit

Say:

The caregiver workflow is intentionally simple: start the visit, complete the checklist, add a note, and end the visit.

## Family Update — 35 seconds

Show:

- Family home
- Visit update
- Raise concern
- Weekly report

Say:

After checkout, the family receives a clean update and can raise a concern without calling the agency repeatedly.

## Incident Safety — 25 seconds

Show:

- Incident queue
- Requires review
- Safe family wording

Say:

Sensitive incidents go to agency review first. Raw high-severity details are not automatically sent to families.

## Close — 15 seconds

Say:

CareProof is ready for a controlled 30-day pilot with a small home care agency.
