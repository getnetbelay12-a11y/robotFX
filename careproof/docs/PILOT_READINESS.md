# CareProof Pilot Readiness

CareProof is pilot-ready when a small home care agency can use it for 2 to 4 weeks without daily developer intervention.

## Required workflow

1. Admin creates caregiver, client, family member, care plan, and visit.
2. Caregiver checks in, completes checklist, adds a note, and checks out.
3. Incident and family concern handling works without exposing unsafe details.
4. Family feed shows clean visit updates.
5. Weekly reports can be generated and exported.

## Pilot readiness gates

- Backend, admin, and mobile builds pass.
- Seeded demo environment looks operational, not empty.
- Health and readiness endpoints are live.
- Role access and agency isolation are covered by tests.
- Notification records are created for critical workflow events.
- CSV import templates exist for onboarding.
- Backup, monitoring, and legal notes are documented.

## Pilot support stance

- Email support is required.
- WhatsApp or SMS support is acceptable for pilot agencies.
- Do not promise emergency response behavior.
